## 关于天筹求解器 (About OptVerse)

**华为天筹求解器 (OptVerse)** 是一款由华为独立研发的高性能数学规划求解器，专注于解决大规模线性规划 (LP)、混合整数线性规划 (MILP) 、二次规划 (QP) 、二次锥规划（SOCP）、一般非线性规划（NLP）及约束规划（CP）等复杂问题。通过融合经典运筹学算法与前沿 AI 技术，为供应链优化、生产调度、交通物流等场景提供极致的求解效率。

本项目的愿景是提供一系列围绕数学优化求解器的易用性工具，与社区共同打造更加高效、智能的数学优化求解器生态。

**Huawei OptVerse Solver** is a high-performance solver developed by Huawei. It excels at solving complex mathematical programming problems, including LP, MILP, QP, SOCP, NLP, and CP. By fusing classical OR algorithms with AI techniques, it provides efficient solutions for real-world applications such as supply chain, production, and logistics optimization.

Our mission is to offer user-friendly tools that complement OptVerse, building a faster and smarter optimization ecosystem alongside the community.
